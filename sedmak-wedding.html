Sedmak Wedding

Art Direction, Design, Web Development

Getting married was one of the happiest days and most memorable moments of my life. Prior to the big day, I was happy to design and concept the wedding invitations for all of our terrific guests. I designed these wedding invitations to be a unique experience upon opening them from the mail. Included are a formal invitation, response card, folded map, and information card — where guests can learn more about our story, the wedding party, hotels, and gift registry via website.
